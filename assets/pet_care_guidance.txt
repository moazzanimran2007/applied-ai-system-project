General pet care tips:
- Regular feeding at consistent times helps digestion and behavior.
- Short, frequent walks are better than one long walk for many dogs.
- Cats benefit from environmental enrichment like puzzle feeders and vertical spaces.
- Medication should be given on schedule; set reminders for time-sensitive meds.
- If a task is missed, reschedule it for the next available window rather than skipping permanently.

Scheduling notes:
- Blocked time windows (lunch, work meetings) should be avoided when planning care tasks.
- Prioritize required tasks (meds, feeding) before optional enrichment if time is limited.

Owner preferences:
- Owners often prefer walks and feeding to happen early or late in the day depending on work schedules.
